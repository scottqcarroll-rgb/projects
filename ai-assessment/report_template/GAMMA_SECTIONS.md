# Gamma Template Structure
## AI Assessment Report — 6 Slides

Matches the 4-phase process from the Excalidraw diagram.

---

### Slide 1 — Executive Summary
- Client name, business type, date
- Top 3 pain points (bullet list)
- **Projected Outcome:** X hours/week reclaimed | $Y/month recovered

---

### Slide 2 — Effort vs. Impact Matrix
- 2×2 grid visual
- Quick Wins (LOW effort / HIGH impact) — highlighted in green
- Big Bets (HIGH effort / HIGH impact)
- Each tool plotted as a labeled dot

---

### Slide 3 — Recommended Solutions (one card per tool)
For each of 5–7 tools:
- Tool name + logo
- Problem it solves
- 3-step setup
- Monthly cost
- Hours saved/week

---

### Slide 4 — 4-Day Quick Win Plan
- Day 1: [Tool] — [Action]
- Day 2: [Tool] — [Action]
- Day 3: [Tool] — [Action]
- Day 4: Connect + Test

---

### Slide 5 — What's Next (Upsell Menu)
Sourced from Excalidraw diagram:
- **Process Redesign** ($3–5k) — Fix current process before automating the waste
- **Automation Build** ($1–3k) — Build, test, hand off. No babysitting.
- **Knowledge Systems** ($3k+) — Custom GPT based on your contact history
- **Custom Workflows** ($3–5k) — Standardize operations
- **Full Implementation** ($5–10k) — Configure all agents, full workflow automation

---
