# Gamma Template — Exact Slide Structure
## Based on audittemplate.ai (8 slides)

---

### Slide 1 — Cover
- Title: "AI Tools Assessment"
- Subtitle: "Prepared for [CLIENT NAME]"
- Date: "Assessment Date: [DATE]"
- Dark background, centered white text

---

### Slide 2 — Executive Summary
Three sections:
- **Pain** — 1–2 sentences describing the core time/friction problems
- **Outcome** — 1 sentence: "Targeted tool additions... can return X+ hours per week by..."
- **The Opportunity at a Glance** — Big number in a circle: "[X] Hours You Can Reclaim Weekly"
- Subtext: "Primary Focus: [Efficiency (Time Savings) / Revenue Growth / etc.]"

---

### Slide 3 — Impact-Effort Matrix
2×2 grid layout:
```
              High Impact
    Quick Wins  |  Major Projects
Low Effort -----+------ High Effort
    Ignore These|  Fill-ins
              Low Impact
```

Below the matrix — "Quick Wins (High Impact, Low Effort)" bullet list:
- [Pain point] → [Tool recommendation in one line]
- [Pain point] → [Tool recommendation in one line]
- [Pain point] → [Tool recommendation in one line]
- [Pain point] → [Tool recommendation in one line]

---

### Slide 4 — Recommended Solutions
Numbered cards (one per tool), each card contains:
- **Number** (1, 2, 3, 4...)
- **Problem Title** (e.g. "No consistent meeting notes or action item capture")
- **Recommended Tool:** [Tool Name]
- **Why This Fits:** [1–2 sentences explaining the fit]
- **Complexity:** plug-and-play OR some-setup OR complex
- **Monthly Cost:** $X (or Free)
- **Setup Time:** X minutes / X hours
- **Time Saved:** X hours/week

---

### Slide 5 — Your 4-Day Quick Wins Plan
2×2 grid of day boxes:
- **Day 1** — Task: [Action] | Tool: [Tool name]
- **Day 2** — Task: [Action] | Tool: [Tool name]
- **Day 3** — Task: [Action] | Tool: [Tool name]
- **Day 4** — Task: [Action] | Tool: [Tool name]

---

### Slide 6 — What Comes After Quick Wins
3 columns (01, 02, 03) — upsell opportunities:
- **01** — [Upsell description with tool bolded]
- **02** — [Upsell description with tool bolded]
- **03** — [Upsell description with tool bolded]

Examples from template:
- "Set up **GoHighLevel CRM** to consolidate leads and streamline sales communication"
- "Create a **Brand Voice Claude Skill** to consistently create content in your voice/style"
- "Set up a **Custom GPT** for [industry] customers to handle FAQs"

---

### Slide 7 — Financial Impact
Two big numbers side by side:
- Left: "Weekly Time Returned" → **[X] hours**
- Right: "Monthly Net ROI" → **$[Y]**
- Below: "Total monthly tool cost: $[Z]"

Formula: (hours/week × 4 weeks × $100/hr) − monthly tool costs = Net ROI

---

### Slide 8 — Your Next Steps
Two numbered action items:
1. **Implement the 4 Quick Wins** — "Follow the plan exactly as outlined to reclaim time and stabilize operations."
2. **Schedule a 30-minute Review Call** — "We'll review results, validate wins, and decide if deeper automation is warranted."

CTA button: "Schedule Your Review Call" → [booking link]
